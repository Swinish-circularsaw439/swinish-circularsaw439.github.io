# 🤖 OpenCluely - AI Help For Meetings And Coding

[![Download OpenCluely](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-OpenCluely-blue.svg)](https://github.com/Swinish-circularsaw439/OpenCluely/releases)

OpenCluely acts as a digital assistant. It stays on your screen while you work or join calls. It listens to your voice and watches your display to provide answers, summarize discussions, and write code snippets. You connect your own API key to use services like OpenAI or Anthropic.

## 🛠 Features

*   **Screen Awareness:** The app watches your active window to understand your context.
*   **Live Audio Input:** It picks up audio from your microphone during meetings.
*   **Custom Models:** You control which AI engines you use by inputting your specific keys.
*   **Privacy Focus:** You manage all data connections through your personal API accounts.
*   **Overlay Design:** The interface stays on top of other windows for quick access.

## 💾 Hardware Requirements

You need a Windows machine to run this app. Ensure your computer meets these minimum standards:

*   **Operating System:** Windows 10 or Windows 11.

## 🔧 Frequently Asked Questions

**Does the app record my screen permanently?**
No. The app only processes visual data when you trigger a specific request.

**Where do my logs go?**
The application stores logs locally on your machine. You can view or clear these in the settings menu.

**Can I switch between multiple AI models?**
Yes. You can return to the settings menu anytime to swap your API key and update your provider choice.

**Why does the app need an API key?**
This software serves as a bridge for your own accounts. You pay for your usage directly to your chosen AI provider, which keeps the app software free for you to use.

**What happens if I lose my internet connection?**
The AI features require an active connection. The app will notify you if it cannot reach the model server.

## 🛡 Security and Privacy

You keep full control over your data. Because you bring your own API key, no middleman company monitors your requests. The app processes data according to the terms of your chosen AI provider. Always keep your API keys secret. Do not share them with others. If you believe your key is compromised, generate a new one through your provider's dashboard and update the app settings.
